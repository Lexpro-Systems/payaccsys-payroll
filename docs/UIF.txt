UIF CALCULATIONS
================

- Caclulated on gross remuneration of the employee with the following exclusions:

    - Non employment related remuneration. Eg: Pensions, annuities and payments to a labour broker in possession of an exemption certificate.
    - Any amount paid for loss of employment, such as retrenchment pay and payment for commutation of office.
    - Once off payments such as Lump Sum Payments from pension, provident and retirement annuity funds.
    - Restraint of trade payments.
    - Commission
    
- The amount is split into two equal parts between the employee and the employer.
- Current amount is 2% of employee gross remuneration with above exclusions taken into account.
- There is a maximum amount of income that attract UIF.  The maximums are as follows
    - R178 464 per year
    - R 14 872 per month
    - R  3 432 per week
    - R  6 864 fortnightly
