Exceptions Report

We've added a process paryun screen which includes a display of exceptions, warning the user of possible mistakes on the payrun before it is processed. The payrun may only be processed once all exceptions have either been corrected or checked. Exceptions can be checked individually, by clicking on the check next to the exception, or collectively, by clicking on the check in the heading of the exceptions list which will check all exceptions at once.

Email Payrun Payslips

An 'Email Payslips' item has been added to the options menu () of each of the listed payruns on the Payruns screen, enabling you to re-send payslips to employees via email if the payrun has already been processed. This option can also be accessed via the options menu () of the 'Edit Payrun' screen.

Payrun options menu.

Payslip Heading Colour

You can now customise your payslips even further by changing the heading colours. To do so, simply go to the 'Payslip Setup' screen in the 'Setup' section, and click on the 'Edit' link next to the 'Template Settings' heading.
