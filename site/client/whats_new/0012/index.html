Bursaries & Scholarships

We've added once-off payslip items for Non-taxable Basic Education Bursaries (SARS code 3815) as well as Non-taxable Further Education Bursaries (SARS code 3821).

Please note that the aforementioned items should only be used for bursaries to employees and NOT for bursaries to the family members of employees as such bursaries may include a taxable component. Functionality for bursaries to family members of employees will be added in the future.
