Tax Reconciliations

The 'IRP5 Certificates' menu item has been replaced with the 'Tax Reconciliations' menu item. All the relevant tax certificates will now be generated as part of a reconciliation.

Tax Reconciliations menu item.

SARS Exports

Tax reconciliations can now be exported in CSV format for import in SARS e@syFile™. Access the SARS export functionality via the the menu () of the reconciliation you wish to export or via the 'SARS Export' button when viewing a reconciliation.

SARS export menu item.

Export Bank Payments

You can now also export the net pay of employees in a given payrun as bank payments in Bankserv (ACB) or CSV format. Simply select the 'Bank Payments Export' option in the menu () of the relevant payrun to open a wizard which will guide you through the export process.

Export bank payments menu item.
