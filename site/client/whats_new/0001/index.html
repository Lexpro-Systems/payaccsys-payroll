Employee Payslip Section

A payslip section has been added to the employee screen. In this section you can view all the employee's payslips, download them or email them to the employee.

New payslip section.

1. Viewing Payslips

Click on the value in the 'Date' column to view the payslip's details.

2. Downloading Payslips

Payslips can be downloaded in PDF format. There are two ways to download a payslip:

  • View the payslip and click on the 'Download' button in the top rigth corner.
  • Click on the options menu () in list of payslips and select the 'Download' option.

3. Emailing Payslips

Payslips can be emailed to any email address. There are two ways to email the payslip:

  • View the payslip and click on the 'Email To' button in the top rigth corner.
  • Click on the options menu () in list of payslips and select the 'Email To' option.
