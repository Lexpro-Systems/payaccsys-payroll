Payslip Leave

Lexpro Payroll calculates leave on a daily basis and prior to this update the leave displayed on the payslip reflected the leave available to the employee as on the date the payslip was downloaded and/or emailed.

Henceforth the leave displayed on the payslip will be calculated to always reflect the leave available to the employee at the payslip end date.

Other Non-Taxable Allowances

Added a payslip item for other non-taxable allowances. This item will be reflected under code 3714 on tax certificates.

Self-Service Invitations

Implemented functionality to invite employees to use the "Self-Service" portal which gives them functionality such as viewing and downloading their payslips, viewing leave and attendance history, requesting leave, and more. Employees will receive an email containing a link to the self-service portal that will allow them to register an account, if they wish to do so.
