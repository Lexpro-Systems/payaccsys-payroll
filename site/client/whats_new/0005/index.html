Lexpro Accounting Integration

It is now possible to post payroll transactions to Lexpro Accounting. Here's how:

1. Connect to your Lexpro Accounting bookset

Go to the 'Accounting Setup' tab of the 'Setup' section and click on the 'Connect' button. Complete your Lexpro Accounting login details in the panel that will appear and click on the 'Login' button to connect your Lexpro Accounting bookset.

Connect to Lexpro Accounting.

2. Setup your Lexpro Accounting configuration

Once you have connected you Lexpro Accounting bookset, click on the 'Configuration' button to specify the accounts that the payroll transactions should be posted to.

Lexpro Accounting configuration.

3. Post your payrun transactions to Lexpro Accounting

Click on the menu () next to any processed payrun in the 'Payroll' section and select the 'Post to Lexpro Accounting' option to display a panel that will allow you to preview and post the payrun transactions to Lexpro Accounting.

Post to Lexpro Accounting.

Retirement Funds

We've expanded our retirement fund functionality to allow you to setup provident funds with either fixed or percentage contributions for employers and employees. Setup your provident funds in the following steps:

1. Add a provident fund

Go to the 'Retirement Fund Setup' tab of the 'Setup' section and click on the 'Add' button to display a panel that will allow you to add a provident fund.

Add a provident fund.

2. Select employees as members of the provident fund

First, go to the 'Employees' section and click on the name of the employee you wish to add to the provident fund to view the employee details. Next, select the 'Retirement Funds' tab. Finally, select the provident fund the employee is a member of by clicking on the checkbox next to the name of the fund.

Select an employee as a member of a provident fund.

IRP5 Certificates

Functionality have been implemented to generate IRP5 certificates for employees. Certificates can be viewed, downloaded, or sent to employees via email. IRP5 certificates can be accessed either via the 'IRP5 Certificates' option in the main menu or the 'IRP5 Certificates' tab when viewing an employee's details.
