Enable/Disable Taxable Benefit on Low Interest or Interest Free Loans

A taxable benefit shall be deemed to have been granted to the employee if a loan is given and either no interest is payable by the employee or interest is payable at a lower interest rate in comparison to the official rate of interest.

In the past, the taxable benefit was calculated and added to the employee's payslip automatically. However, since there are some exceptions to the rule (e.g., under certain conditions when the value of the loan is R3,000 or less), the functionality has been implemented to enable or disable the taxable benefit calculation at the user's discretion.

Enable or disable taxable benefit when adding a loan.

The taxable benefit calculation may also be enabled or disabled for existing loans going to the "Loans" section from the main menu, clicking on the loan description to view the loan, and clicking on the "Edit" button.

Enable or disable taxable benefit when editing a loan.
