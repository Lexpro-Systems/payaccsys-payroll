Year-End PAYE Corrections

We've added functionality for year-end PAYE corrections. On the last month of the financial year (February) the total amount of PAYE will be calculated for each employee. If the total PAYE for the employee is greater than the prescribed amount the difference will be deducted from the final month's PAYE.

This feature may be enabled/disabled per employee. Please note that the feature will be enabled by default for new employees (see the screenshot below) but will NOT be enabled for current employees. To enable the feature for current employees, please edit the employee income tax details accordingly.

Enable year-end PAYE correction when adding an employee.

Add Payruns By Department

Payruns can now be created by department. Simply select the department from the dropdown list when adding a payrun to create a payrun for all the employees in the specified department. Please note that creating a payrun for All Departments will include only those employees who have not already been included in another payrun for the same period (e.g., when creating a payrun for the Sales and Marketing department for a given period and then creating another payrun for All Departments for the same period, the All Departments payrun will only include those employees not already in the Sales and Marketing payrun).

Employee Tax Certificate Access

Employees will now be able to view or download tax certificates at the employer's discretion. Follow these simple steps to give employees access to their tax certificates:

Step 1:

Click on the Self Service Access button when viewing the Tax Certificates for a given Tax Reconciliation.

Give employee access to tax certificates.

Step 2:

Select the tax certificates you wish to make available by checking them (click on the orange check button in the heading bar to select/unselect all) and click on the Apply button.

Select certificates to give access to.

The employee will now be able to view or download the tax certificates by clicking on the Tax Certificates item in the main menu of the employee portal.

View tax certificates in the employee portal.
