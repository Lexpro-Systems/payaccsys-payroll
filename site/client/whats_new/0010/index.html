UIF Cap Updated

With effect from 1 June 2021 the UIF cap has been increased to R212,544 annually. These changes will be reflected automatically in all payslips dated on or after 1 June 2021.

End Employment Reason

Employers will now be able to specify the reason for ending the employement of an employee.

End Employment Reason

Updated Fringe Benefits

'General Fringe Benefits' and 'Free / Cheap Accommodation' fringe benefits have been added (corresponding to SARS codes 3801 and 3805 respectively).

Resubmit Leave Requests

Leave requests may now be resubmitted to the leave administrator for approval or declination by clicking on the 'Resubmit' option in the menu () on the right side of the listed leave request.

'Resubmit' leave request menu item.

Highlight Public Holidays

Public holidays will be highlighted in red when adding or editing leave requests (also in the Self Service Portal).

Edit Attendance Entries

An option has been implemented to edit an attendance entry from the attendance histroy panel. Click on the menu icon () to the right of the entry you wish to edit and select the 'Edit' item to do so.

'Edit' attendance menu item.

Self Service Portal: Attendance History

We've added a section to the Self Service Portal allowing users to view or export their attendance history. Exports can be done in either 'Excel' or 'CSV' format.
